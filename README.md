# Zurura

Zurura is a transport management and tracking platform designed to provide real-time visibility into the movement of vehicles through GPS tracking and centralized monitoring. The system enables administrators, fleet managers and transport operators to monitor vehicle locations, manage transport operations and improve efficiency through a unified digital interface.

The project focuses on live GPS tracking, operational transparency and intelligent transport oversight, allowing organizations to observe and manage vehicle movement in real time.

---

## Project Purpose

Transportation systems often suffer from limited visibility into vehicle movements, inefficient coordination, and lack of reliable operational data. Zurura addresses these challenges by providing a live monitoring platform for vehicles that integrates GPS tracking with a management dashboard.

The system allows transport operators to:

- Track vehicles in real time using GPS data
- Monitor fleet movement and operational status
- Improve accountability and transparency in transport operations
- Optimize route usage and dispatch decisions
- Maintain a centralized view of transport activity

Zurura acts as the digital control center for transport operations, helping organizations move from manual oversight to data-driven fleet management.

---

## Project Goal

The primary goal of Zurura is to build a reliable and scalable transport management platform that enables real-time vehicle monitoring and operational control.

Key objectives include:

- Provide live GPS tracking of vehicles
- Build a centralized platform for transport monitoring
- Improve operational visibility for fleet managers
- Enable data collection for analytics and optimization
- Support scalable fleet management for organizations of varying sizes

Ultimately, Zurura aims to become a modern infrastructure layer for transport oversight, enabling smarter mobility operations through real-time data.

---

## Core Features

- **Live GPS Tracking**  
  Real-time location tracking of vehicles on a map interface.

- **Fleet Monitoring Dashboard**  
  Central interface for observing vehicle status and movement.

- **Transport Operations Management**  
  Tools for overseeing and coordinating fleet activity.

- **Data Collection & Visibility**  
  Continuous logging of vehicle movement data for monitoring and analysis.
